Posted on 2019-04-25 | Visitors:

I’m a research fellow with Prof. Dong Jin Song at School of Computing, National University of Singapore. I also work closely with Prof. Sun Jun.

I’m interested in problems in the junction of formal methods, software engineering, security and machine learning. In particular, my current research interests are focused on building trustworthy Artificial Intelligence (AI) systems and cyber-physical systems (CPS) using techniques like testing and verification.
